blackcandy is a self hosted music streaming server. In this catalog (Infrastructure), listed as “General OSS”: General с открытым кодом software. Key topics: Audio, Hotwire, Music, Music Player, Music Streaming, and Musicplayer. Project details: free с открытым кодом and built mainly in Ruby.
